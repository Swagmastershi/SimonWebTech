MOTIVATION

SimonWebTech was created because Simon wanted to learn how to develop website in January of 2015, his junior year of high school. The course he signed up for was Udemy's "Complete Web Developer Course" with Rob Percival. Percival's excellent teaching skills in the course allowed Simon to be inspired to be a web developer! Now, Simon wants to share what he likes to do in his free time!
